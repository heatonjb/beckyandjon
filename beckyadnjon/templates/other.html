Getting to London

By car
If you're travelling by car please pre-arrange carparking with your hotel. You can't park on the street in Islington without a residents parking permit.

By train
Islington is only a very short bus, taxi or tube ride away from Euston and Kings Cross National Rail terminals. The closest tube stop to Fredericks is Angel station. Many bus routes also run in this area. To find the best route for your journey use the TFL journey planner
