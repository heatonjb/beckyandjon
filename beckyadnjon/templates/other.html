Getting to London

Car Travel
If your travelling by car to London please pre-arrange carparking with your hotel. You can't park on the street in Islington without a residents parking permit.

Train Travel
Islington is only a very short bus, taxi or tube ride away from Euston and Kings Cross National rail terminals. You can get the Nortern Line to Angel Islington which is two stops from Euston or one stop from Kings Cross. Many bus routes run to Angel from these stations. To find the best one or indeed to find any route across London use the Transport for London journey planner
